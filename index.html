Project Overview

Our Goal

To design and develop a sophisticated, scalable, and user-friendly website dedicated to apartment rentals for the foreign community in Sri Lanka.

Build a flexible platform with the built-in capability to seamlessly integrate hotel listings in a future phase, creating a comprehensive accommodation hub.

Market Analysis

Competitor Research Summary

EK Properties: Sets standard with USD pricing, advanced filters & client login.
Expat Homes: Effectively targets niche with “Expat Only” listings & submission system.
Pearl Sri Lanka: Broadens appeal with bilingual support, legal services & diverse villas.
Olive Apartments: Offers short/long-term rentals and an open "Add Listing" feature.
British Brokers: Builds trust with a clear focus on expats & personalized service.
